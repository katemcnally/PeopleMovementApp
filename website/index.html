Project Description

People Movement is an app for orchestrating the movement of people on foot. Our project targets two user groups: the group organizer and the participant. People Movement allows users to create events for their protests, marches, etc. while the average urban commuter can receive optimized walking directions to avoid large groups of people in their way.
